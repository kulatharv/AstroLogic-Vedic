About AstroLogic

Integrating Vedic Astrology with Artificial Intelligence

Overview

AstroLogic is an AI-integrated astrology platform designed to provide structured, intelligent, and scalable astrological insights. The platform combines traditional Vedic principles with modern backend architecture to deliver reliable and dynamic predictions.

Core Features

The system includes daily horoscope services, Panchang calculations, Kundali generation, and AI-enhanced prediction modules. The architecture supports modular expansion for weekly, monthly, and personalized astrological services.

Technology Stack

AstroLogic is built using FastAPI for backend services, Jinja templates for server-side rendering, and a responsive frontend powered by Bootstrap. Planetary calculations are structured for integration with Swiss Ephemeris, while AI modules enhance interpretation and prediction generation.

Vision

The vision of AstroLogic is to create a scalable, AI-driven astrology ecosystem that bridges classical astrological computation with modern intelligent systems. The platform is designed to evolve toward fully personalized, data-driven astrological analysis.
