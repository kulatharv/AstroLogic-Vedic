Why Vedic

The Science of Jyotish

🌌Sidereal Zodiac

Vedic astrology uses the actual positions of constellations (sidereal), not the tropical zodiac that has drifted ~24° from the sky. Our calculations use the precise Lahiri Ayanamsa correction.

⏱Vimshottari Dasha

The 120-year planetary period system maps life chapters to exact planet cycles. Every major life event — marriage, job change, illness — correlates with a Mahadasha or Antardasha transition.

🏠12 House System

Each house governs a specific life domain. The lord of each house and planets occupying it determine your experiences in wealth, relationships, career, health and spiritual growth.

⭐27 Nakshatras

The Moon's 27 lunar mansions provide granular personality and timing detail not available in Western astrology. Your birth nakshatra determines your Dasha starting point and core nature.
